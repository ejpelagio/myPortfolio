    Erik J Pelagio

    Erik Pelagio is a Full stack Web developer. His main interest in programming developed through his enjoyment in solving problems, trouble shooting and using his creativity to create functional programs. After years of spending time in the Orthodontics field he decided to attend The University of Texas at Austin to complete an intensive and thorough boot camp to become a Web Developer. Here he learned to work with a variety of different programs proficiently.

    Eriks favorite part of programming is when he sees the final result of all his work come together. He takes pride in all his projects because he knows that he he has perfected every possible to his imagination.

    His Experience incudes but not limited to HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Node.js, Express, Bootstrap, Materialize, Firebase, MySQL, API/JSON, RESTful API, Ajax, State Management, Active Record, and Command Line.

    When not in front of a screen Erik enjoys spending time with his wife Tania and two kids, Gianna and Erik. They find joy in taking walks around the neighborhood or on a warm sunny day out at Zilker Park in Austin. Sports, especially football have always been a part of Eriks heart. Erik volunteers as a head coach with the Texas Youth Football Association in Pflugerville, Texas. Since 2010 he has coached children ages three to ten including his own Son Erik Jr.
